Learning Plan

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

To become a Full Stack Web Developer and eventually move toward a Team Leading role, where I could manage a small team focused on developing new tools or technologies. I want to grow not just as a developer but also as a leader, helping guide a team through challenges and shaping innovative projects.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

My biggest strength when it comes to learning is definitely my curiosity. I genuinely enjoy exploring new topics and diving deep into things that catch my interest. This helps me stay motivated, especially when I'm learning something challenging or unfamiliar. I tend to take the initiative to look beyond the immediate material and discover more, which often leads me to unexpected insights or solutions.
On the flip side, one of my limitations is a lack of discipline. I sometimes struggle to get started on tasks, especially when they feel repetitive or unexciting. My tendency to seek out new experiences can cause me to lose focus on the fundamentals or tasks that require patience. But I believe with enough practice, I can improve my discipline and find ways to balance my excitement with consistency in my learning.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

One of the key skills I'd like to develop at Dev Academy is better communication, especially when working with teams. I want to get better at clearly expressing my ideas and collaborating more effectively with others, whether it's during problem-solving or just day-to-day interactions.
Additionally, I want to build greater discipline. since I struggle sometimes with it, so developing a stronger sense of discipline would help me stay consistent and productive.
